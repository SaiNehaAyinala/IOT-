Automatic Dustbin

Now a days, it has emerged as an emergency to keep surroundings clean and be safe. The main motto of our project is to help in the former case. It is very unhygienic process for one to clean the dustbins,so our IOT model helps in automatically emptying the dustbins according to the moisture of dust and distance of the dustbin carrier. This helps daily wage cleaners in such a way that they themselves can be clean while cleaning our dustbins.

ABOUT

The Automatic Dustbin works based on IoT.
It sends notifications if we do not follow the categorized dustbin to throw the dust.
This helps the minicipal depatment to check correctly while collecting the dust and also helps the user to follow it properly.
Automatic notification will be sent and also using contact page the street members can write a message if they have any problem.
Worry less,as we take care of your health.

Services

Customised Notification.

Categorization.
Municipality dept contact.
